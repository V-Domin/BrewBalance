About Me

This is what I do.

Hi there,

My name is Vitaliy and I'm an entry-level developer who is passionate about coding and programming. I've been studying programming as a hobby for some time now, and I'm always looking to learn more. Currently, I work as an English teacher for adults, but my ultimate goal is to combine my love for teaching and coding one day.

In my free time, I love experimenting with new programming languages and techniques, and I'm always trying to challenge myself to learn something new. I believe that programming is a powerful tool that can help us solve complex problems and improve our lives in countless ways.

As someone who has experienced the challenges of balancing work, personal interests, and self-care, I created this website to share my insights and methods for staying balanced. I believe that mindfulness and self-care are essential for anyone pursuing a demanding career or hobby, and I hope that my experiences and tips can help others find more balance and joy in their lives.

Thank you for visiting BrewBalance website, and I hope you find something here that inspires you on your own journey of learning and growth.
